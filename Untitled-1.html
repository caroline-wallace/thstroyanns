About

The Trinity Troy-Anns  are an award winning drill team. They were established in 1969. The sweethearts celebrate the best that Trinity High School has to offer. We combine discipline with creativity. As the saying goes....Once a Troy-Ann always a Troy-Ann.

  Madeline Quiroga 

Madeline Quiroga is the head director of the Trinity High School Troy-Anns. Coach Quiroga was captain of the team in 2014. Since graduating she attended Texas Tech University and earned a Bachelor's degree in psychology. She also recieved a master's degree from Texas Woman's University in Psychology. During this time Ms. Quiroga was working as a dance teacher at a private studio , training students in multiple styles of dance. These styles included jazz, tap, hip-hop, clogging, and contemporary,                                                              

Megan Gibson 

Megan Gibson is the assistant director of the Trinity High School Troy-Anns. Ms. Gibson was a line member in 2012. Upon graduation she attended the University of Arkansas. Ms. Gibson is the mother of an adorable 8 month old little boy, Merrick.
